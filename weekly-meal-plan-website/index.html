Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about fuelling your fitness journey with precision nutrition

How does the weekly meal plan work?

Each week you'll receive a tailored meal plan to suit your individual metrics including body composition, activity level, and fitness goals.

Your weekly plan includes macro-balanced meals, snack suggestions, shopping lists, and meal prep instructions delivered straight to your app every week.

How often do I need to cook and meal prep?

Our plans are designed to be time-efficient and manageable. You'll cook on Sunday to prepare your breakfasts plus meal prep for the next 3 days.

Dinners are made fresh each night (or you can batch cook for 2 nights at once), then you'll meal prep again on Wednesday for the rest of the week. This approach keeps your meals fresh while minimising daily cooking time.

How do I time my nutrition around training sessions?

Nutrient timing is crucial for maximising performance and recovery. We provide specific guidance on when to eat carbohydrates, proteins, and fats relative to your workouts.

This includes pre-workout fuelling strategies, intra-workout nutrition for longer sessions, and post-workout recovery protocols to optimise muscle protein synthesis.

Can I cancel my weekly plan anytime?

Absolutely! There's no long-term commitment required. You can cancel your weekly meal plan at any time through your account settings.

We also offer a 14-day money-back guarantee, so if you're not completely satisfied with your first week, we'll refund your payment in full.

What if I have dietary restrictions or allergies?

While we don't create separate meal plans for different dietary requirements, we provide comprehensive swap guides to help you substitute proteins and other ingredients to suit your needs.

We encourage you to make adjustments where necessary so the plan fits perfectly with your preferences and dietary requirements, ensuring you can still achieve your goals while eating foods you enjoy.

How quickly will I see results from following the plan?

Most people notice improved energy levels and workout performance within the first week of following their tailored nutrition plan.

Body composition changes typically become noticeable after 2-3 weeks of consistent adherence, with ongoing improvements as you continue with your weekly meal plans and proper training.

Do you provide support if I have questions about my plan?

Yes! We provide ongoing support to help you succeed with your nutrition journey. Our team is available to answer questions about meal planning, macro calculations, and optimisation strategies.

You'll also have access to our community of like-minded athletes who share tips, recipes, and motivation to help you stay on track week after week.
